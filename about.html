WHO ARE WE?

We are the Research and Development Center at Life University, Sihanoukville, Cambodia, dedicated to advancing the fields of Application Development and Cybersecurity. Our mission is to provide students in the Computer Science program with cutting-edge knowledge and practical experience, empowering them to meet global standards in technology.

At the heart of our vision is a commitment to shaping students into future leaders who can excel in their studies, open doors to international opportunities, and secure promising careers. We aim to inspire innovation, foster critical thinking, and prepare our students to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.
